Over 300 acres of agave fields, with our own nursery staying sustainable

FORGED IN GUERRERO

Our Agave farming practices are sustainable and environmentally friendly, we plant at least 10 agaves for everyone that is harvested.

We practice polyculture planting, intermixing, Agave angustifolia (Espadin), Agave cupreata (Papalometl) and wild native angustifolia on the same fields at the same time, imitating the diversity found in the natural ecosystem, helping with natural pest control.

CRAFTED IN TANDEM WITH NATURE

We have installed bat houses, since bats are the primary agave pollinators, and bee houses help our local bee population with flower pollination. Additionally, we utilize sheep to help with weed control and natural fertilization, striving to be truly organic.

Distillery building

Made from 100% NATURAL stone from our farm

OVEN

Our oven is a conical artisanal oven, 100% volcanic rock.

We chose natural stone from our farm since it is the original organic building material not requiring chemicals for production, and produced minimal excess material, and highly recyclable.

The stone was extracted and not artificially produced to build our distillery. By extracting the stone, our workers hand removed the and collected surface stone, cutting it and polishing it. It was not mined or chemically produced, which both destroy the natural environment.
